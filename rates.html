Lesson Rates

We keep our tuition straightforward: all lessons are charged per hour- no extra fees for enrollment. Your first meeting is a trial lesson, after which students commit to a semester of weekly lessons to ensure continuity and progress. Contact us for pricing.

OfferingDescription
Trial LessonOne 60‑minute introductory lesson; no obligation.
Semester TuitionFifteen 60‑minute weekly lessons (one semester).

Semester tuition may be paid in full at the beginning of the term or in equal monthly installments. We accept cash, check, and major credit cards. Please see our Cancellation & Refund Policy for details about make‑ups and refunds.
