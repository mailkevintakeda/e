Instructor Bios

Kevin Takeda

Kevin Takeda is a DMA candidate at the Cleveland Institute of Music and a graduate of The Juilliard School. He has extensive teaching experience with students of all ages and levels—from beginners building fundamentals to advanced pianists preparing auditions, competitions, and conservatory study.

His teaching emphasizes healthy, efficient technique, musical clarity, and expressive freedom. Lessons take place in a focused home studio environment featuring a full-sized Steinway grand piano.
